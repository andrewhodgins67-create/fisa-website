Agency Mandate
FISA is a federal-level investigative and intelligence agency responsible for safeguarding national infrastructure and enforcing federal statutes related to security, cyber operations, and organized criminal activity.
Core Responsibilities

• Counter-terrorism operations and threat neutralization
• Cybercrime and digital forensics investigations
• Federal corruption and internal misconduct inquiries
• Organized crime disruption and surveillance operations
• Intelligence collection and analysis support
Operational Structure

FISA operates through decentralized task forces embedded within regional jurisdictions, enabling rapid response capability and intelligence sharing across divisions.
