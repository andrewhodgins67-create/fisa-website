Mission Overview
The Critical Incident Response Group (CIRG) provides immediate tactical response to high-risk federal incidents requiring containment, stabilization, and rapid operational control. CIRG is activated during critical threats, large-scale emergencies, hostage scenarios, and situations that exceed standard field division capabilities.
Operational Focus Areas
• Active threat response operations • Emergency incident containment • Hostage and crisis situations • Tactical perimeter control • Critical infrastructure protection • Rapid deployment support operations • Federal emergency coordination
Response Protocol
1. Incident detection and classification 2. Threat level confirmation 3. Deployment authorization 4. Scene containment and stabilization 5. Tactical operations execution 6. Transfer to investigative authority
Operational Notes
CIRG operates as the first-response federal escalation unit for high-risk incidents requiring immediate tactical intervention.
