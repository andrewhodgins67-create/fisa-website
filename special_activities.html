Mission Overview
The Special Activities Center conducts covert intelligence operations requiring strict operational security, compartmentalization, and limited-access execution. The division focuses on intelligence collection through undercover assignments, surveillance operations, and sensitive field deployments designed to operate without detection while producing actionable intelligence.
Operational Focus Areas
• Covert intelligence operations • Undercover field assignments • Surveillance and reconnaissance • Intelligence source development • Classified target monitoring • Digital intelligence collection • High-risk field infiltration
Operational Structure
1. Intelligence requirement intake 2. Mission assignment and compartmentalization 3. Covert field deployment 4. Continuous intelligence reporting 5. Secure data analysis and archiving
Operational Notes
All SAC operations are restricted under need-to-know protocol to prevent exposure of active intelligence assets.
